About the Page

AegisDesk AI is built to demonstrate how real operational teams handle incidents from start to finish. Users submit tickets with a title and description. The system captures priority, category, and status, supports technician assignment, and stores work notes so every action is traceable. It also provides AI-assisted triage (offline heuristic engine) to generate a short incident summary and suggest who should handle the ticket. The goal is to maintain a clean, auditable record of operational work— similar to tools like ServiceNow or Jira Service Management—while keeping everything lightweight and fast.
