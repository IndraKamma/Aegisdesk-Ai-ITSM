About AegisDesk AI

AegisDesk AI is a professional enterprise ticketing and incident management system designed for real-world IT environments including:

  • Data Centers
  • Network Operations Centers (NOC)
  • Cloud Infrastructure Teams
  • IT Support Teams

This system allows teams to create tickets, assign technicians, track status, log troubleshooting steps, and maintain complete audit history.

The goal is to simulate real enterprise tools like:

  • ServiceNow
  • Jira Service Management
  • Microsoft Azure Incident Management
  • Enterprise Help Desk Systems
